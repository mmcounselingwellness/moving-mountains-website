About Moving Mountains Counseling & Wellness

Therapy that meets you where you are

We believe healing is a journey—one that doesn't have to be taken alone. Whether in our Princeton office, virtually across Massachusetts, or walking side by side on a wooded trail, we're here to meet you where you are and help guide you forward.

Our name comes from the belief that while life's challenges can feel overwhelming, they are not immovable. With the right support, even the heaviest burdens can shift—one small step at a time.

We offer compassionate, evidence-based therapy for anxiety, depression, grief, life transitions, burnout, and more through in-person, telehealth, and nature-based sessions.
