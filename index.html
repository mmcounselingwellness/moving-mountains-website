About Moving Mountains Counseling & Wellness

Therapy that meets you where you are

We believe healing is a journey—one that doesn't have to be taken alone. Whether you visit our Princeton office near Wachusett Mountain (just 15 minutes from Worcester and Holden, upstairs at Mountainside Cafe), connect virtually from anywhere in Massachusetts, or walk side by side on a Wachusett trail, we're here to meet you where you are and help guide you forward.

Our name comes from the belief that while life's challenges can feel overwhelming, they are not immovable. With the right support, even the heaviest burdens can shift—one small step at a time.

We offer compassionate, evidence-based therapy for anxiety treatment, depression counseling, grief support, life transitions, burnout recovery, and trauma healing. Our Princeton MA office serves Worcester, Holden, West Boylston, Sterling, Rutland, and surrounding Central Massachusetts communities. We also provide telehealth counseling throughout Massachusetts and nature-based therapy sessions near Wachusett Mountain.
