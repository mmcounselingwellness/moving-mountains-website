I'm incredibly excited to share that [Kate Allain](kate-bio) and I have launched our outpatient therapy practice, **Moving Mountains Counseling and Wellness**!

Kate and I have known each other for nearly a decade, meeting and working side by side in the hospice realm. Within months of working together—both pre-licensed and running on caffeine and daydreams—Kate turned to me and asked, "So, when are we opening our own practice?!" We laughed it off and filed it away under the mental chart labeled "maybe someday".

In January, after eight years, we revisited that daydream. Since then, there have been countless early-morning emails, after-work FaceTimes, and weekend meetings as our kids played around us. Bit by bit, we slowly pieced our vision together.

By a stroke of luck (and fate), we landed our dream office at the base of Wachusett Mountain, atop [Mountainside Bakery & Cafe](https://www.mountainsidebakerycafe.com/), a spot that has always felt like home to us. In October, "someday" finally happened.

We've added our personal touches to the space and have loved welcoming our clients here. We're proud to have created a space where therapy can happen in the comfort of our cozy office, or out in nature, with the Wachusett trail system just steps from our back door. For those farther away, we also offer virtual sessions so anyone in Massachusetts can access the support they need.

We're excited to expand into a second office in January 2026. We have a few openings for new clients and are accepting names on our waitlist. We're also actively hiring therapists and have plans to integrate other wellness practitioners into our community.

We would love your support as we continue building a healing community in Central Massachusetts. If you feel compelled, please like and follow our [Facebook page](https://www.facebook.com/profile.php?id=61582771273798), share this post, and think of us when you or someone you know is looking for mental health support.

We are beyond grateful. 💙⛰️
